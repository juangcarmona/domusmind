## Is this a task manager?

No. DomusMind models household system state; task management is one capability derived from that model.

## Is this a calendar app?

No. It includes timeline and events, but within a broader operational household model.

## Is this only for families?

The initial domain focus is household and family operations, but the platform approach can generalize.

## Can it be self-hosted?

Yes. Self-hostable infrastructure is a core architectural goal.

## Is AI required?

No. AI is a supporting layer and not required for core modeling and coordination.

## Why not use existing tools?

Existing tools manage fragments. DomusMind models and coordinates the full household system.

## Who owns the data?

Families. Data ownership and privacy are core principles.
