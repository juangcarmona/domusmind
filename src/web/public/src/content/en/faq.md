## Is this a task manager?

No. DomusMind shows what is happening in the house and who is responsible. Tasks are part of that, but responsibility and coordination come first.

## Is this a calendar app?

No. Events are on the family timeline, but the calendar is one piece of a larger picture - routines, responsibilities, and people all connect to it.

## Is this only for families?

It is built for households. The initial focus is families with multiple members and shared responsibilities.

## Can it be self-hosted?

Yes. Running it on your own server is a core goal. Your data stays with you.

## Is AI required?

No. DomusMind works without AI. Assistance features are optional additions, not the foundation.

## Why not use existing tools?

Existing tools manage tasks, time, or lists separately. None of them track who is responsible, who knows what, or how the house is doing as a whole. The coordination still falls to one person. That is the problem DomusMind solves.

## Who owns the data?

The family. Always.

## Is V1 available now?

DomusMind is in active development. The domain model and API are being built now. Follow the project to stay updated.
