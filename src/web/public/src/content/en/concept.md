## Household as a system

A household is not a list of tasks. It is a system of people, time, responsibilities, resources, obligations, and logistics.

## State over tasks

DomusMind stores household facts and state. Tasks emerge from state transitions and responsibilities.

## Shared operational memory

Information should not live in one person’s head. DomusMind creates shared visibility across the family.

## Anticipation over reaction

The platform supports planning across time horizons and detects upcoming obligations before they become urgent.

## Explicit responsibilities

Responsibility domains define ownership with primary and secondary members to reduce single points of failure.

## Frictionless capture

Capture must be easier than remembering: quick input, integrations, and APIs become first-class pathways.

## Privacy and ownership

Household data is private family infrastructure. DomusMind prioritizes data ownership, strong access controls, and self-hosting options.
